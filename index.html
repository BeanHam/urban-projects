Urban Projects

All projects presented here explore various aspects of urban spatial intelligence, with a shared goal of enhancing the quality, integrity, and equity of urban data. Together, they aim to build more reliable, interpretable, and inclusive spatial information systems that support evidence-based urban analysis and decision-making.

Spatial Integration

A framework for integrating multiple spatial datasets into a unified structure. This project focuses on harmonizing geospatial sources and resolving inconsistencies in spatial reference systems, formats, and scales.

Spatial Annotation

A visual labeling tool designed for spatial datasets. It supports interactive annotation, region tagging, and metadata enrichment for geospatial images and maps.

Spatial Disaggregation

A spatial data processing toolkit for disaggregating urban-level data into finer spatial units. It uses geospatial interpolation and boundary mapping for localized analytics.
